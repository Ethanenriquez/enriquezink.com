About Me

Hi! I'm Ethan (pronouns: he/him), born in 2007 in Cebu City. I'm a beginner comic publisher.

My goal is to become an animator and a great fictional storyteller. I'm working on my first graphic novel to build patience and consistency. I'm inspired by cultures, music, and animated stories, and I like experimenting with different tools to create unique work.

I currently use Krita for my projects, and plan to switch to Clip Studio Paint in the future.

If you want to get in touch for anything important or urgent, feel free to Contact me!

I built this website myself—it took a few months, and I'm still a beginner web developer. If there are any issues or bugs in this website, click here the GitHub Issues!
